PCB 线宽与电流的关系

2.50mm（98mi1）6.00A5.10A4.50A
2.00mm（78mil）5.10A4.30A4.00A
1.50mm（59mi1）4.20A3.50A3.20A
1.20mm（47mi1）3.60A3.00A2.70A
1.00mm（40mi1）3.20A2.60A2.30A
0.80mm（32mi1）2.80A2.40A2.00A
0.60mm（24m11）2.30A1.90A1.60A
0.50mm（20mil）2.00A1.70A1.35A
0.40mm（16mil）1.70A1.35A1.10A
0.30mm（12mil）1.30A1.10A0.80A
0.20mm（8mi1）0.90A0.70A0.55A
0.15mm（6mil）0.70A0.50A0.20A
